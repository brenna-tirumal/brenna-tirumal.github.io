Cell & Molecular Biology

Prior to joining SFSU as a computer science student, I worked in the biotechnology and food technology industries. This semester I was thrilled to lend a hand to the Biology department as a Graduate Assistant for BIOL 351 - Cell & Molecular Biology Lab.

Experiments conducted in the lab included primary cell culture, protein purification, DNA isolation, PCR, and DNA sequencing.

I hope to continue to use the skills I gained in the life sciences in my future career as an engineer.
