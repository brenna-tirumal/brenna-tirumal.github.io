About Me


"Food Technology"

I am a first year graduate student in computer science at San Francisco State University, returning to school after 5 years working as a bench researcher in biotechnology and food technology. Since I do not have a background in computer science, I spent this semester learning the basics and exploring opportunities. I am excited to gain a new set of skills that will help me contribute to meaningful research in the future.

My research interests are environment/ecology, food distribution and waste, sustainable agriculture, and human health. I would look forward to utilizing technologies like machine learning and artificial intelligence to tackle challenges in these fields.

I moved to San Francisco in 2006, and I never get tired of roaming around in the city. When I have free time (which is almost never these days!), my favorite thing to do is take a coffee to go from any of the local roasters and walk the SF hills. If the day ends with a martini at a classic hotel bar, it's never a bad thing!
